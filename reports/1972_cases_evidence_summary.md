## Executive Summary

Three unidentified cases from 1972 remain open in British Columbia. **Case 1972-CFU-004** presents the strongest identification potential due to distinctive tattoos, scars, clothing details, and an available RCMP image.

## Recommendation

**1972-CFU-004** should be prioritized for:
1. **DNA re-analysis** with modern techniques
2. **Tattoo database search** - rose and butterfly designs may be searchable
3. **1972 Vancouver missing persons review** - 20-30 male, distinctive appearance
4. **Image enhancement** - RCMP image may benefit from modern enhancement
